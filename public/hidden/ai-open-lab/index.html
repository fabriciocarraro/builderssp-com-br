Our Journey

2023

Aina Kit Launch

The Aina Kit is launched, providing the first set of language resources and tools for underrepresented languages.

2024

7B Model — First Release

The first version of the 7B parameter model is released, marking a milestone in public AI model development.

Late 2024

ALIA Kit Established

The ALIA Kit is established, expanding the suite of public AI resources and tools for language technology development.

2025

40B Model — First Version

The first version of the 40B parameter model is launched, significantly expanding the capabilities of public AI resources.

2026

The AI Open Lab

The AI Open Lab is launched to unify and provide access to all developed public AI resources in a single platform.

Driven by the Barcelona Supercomputing Center

This initiative is driven by the AI Institute of the Barcelona Supercomputing Center – National Supercomputing Center (BSC-CNS), a leading public research institution dedicated to advancing science and technology for the benefit of society.

Beyond providing language models adapted to ecosystem needs, making public resources available enables more ethical and transparent AI — incorporating aspects such as cultural diversity and support for non-global languages.
